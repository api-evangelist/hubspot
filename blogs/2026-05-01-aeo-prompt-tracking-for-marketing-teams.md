You already track and analyze your SEO strategy — keyword rankings, organic traffic, SERP positions. But when a prospect asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews a buying question and your brand doesn’t appear in the answer, traditional rank tracking can’t tell you that. AEO prompt tracking helps you measure brand visibility within AI-generated answers by monitoring whether (and how) your brand gets cited when real AI prompts are run across the engines your audience is actually using.
